### 6. **Polish Your Personal Statement** ⭐ CRITICAL, EASY TO IMPROVE

**Time:** 3-5 hours | **Impact:** VERY HIGH

#### Key Improvements:
1. **Lead with Impact:**
   - "My open source project has 16,000+ weekly downloads..."
   - Not: "I've worked on various projects..."

2. **Use Specific Numbers:**
   - "16,000+ weekly downloads" not "many downloads"
   - "Team of 15 engineers" not "large team"
   - "$150k LOI" not "significant funding"

3. **Connect Everything to UW:**
   - "As Earth Tracker Subsystem Lead for HuskySat-2..."
   - "Working with Dr. Viray in Math AI Lab..."
   - "Through UW's Lavin Entrepreneurship Program..."

4. **Show Growth:**
   - "From entering college at 15 to leading satellite subsystems..."
   - "My academic performance improved from 3.58 to 3.80+..."

5. **End Strong:**
   - Connect all achievements to your CS passion
   - Show why UW CS specifically
   - Demonstrate readiness and commitment

**Why it matters:** This is your #1 opportunity to stand out. Most applicants have good achievements - the personal statement is what makes yours memorable.
